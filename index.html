About Kusanagi

Kusanagi is a community dedicated to anime and gaming. It was founded by three Dominaes: Eren Yeager, the Dominae Imperatix; Lord Grim, the Dominae Asmodeus; and Light Yagami, the Dominae Praefecta. "Kusanagi" is the name of a legendary sword in Japanese mythology. It is also associated with the mythical deity Susanoo, who is said to have found the sword in the tail of an eight-headed serpent.

Dominaes

  • Dominae Imperatrix (Eren Yeager) - The leader in charge of games, competitions, and events within the community. He ensures that all activities are fair, competitive, and enjoyable for all members. He motivates and inspires the Scribentine Vindicator to help organize and run all the community's events.
  • Dominae Asmodeus (Lord Grim) - The leader in charge of the community's ranking system. He ensures that all members are evaluated and ranked fairly based on their contributions and achievements within the community. He motivates and inspires the Rollmaster and Talonarch Vindicators to help maintain and improve the ranking system.
  • Dominae Praefecta (Light Yagami) - The leader in charge of maintaining peace and order within the community. He ensures that all members follow the community's rules and regulations and that conflicts are resolved peacefully and justly. He motivates and inspires the Ironclad Vindicator to help enforce the community's code of conduct and promote a harmonious and respectful environment.

Vindicators

  • Scribentine (Lehm Brick) - The head editor of the community who ensures the accuracy and quality of all written content and defends the community's principles and values.
  • Talonarch (Laura Bodewig) - The head recruiter of the community who seeks out talented individuals to join and defend the community's interests.
  • Ironclad (Lambdadelta) - The one who maintains the peace of the community and enforces the laws and regulations with an unyielding determination.
  • Rollmaster (Lana Lunavince) - The one who collects the attendance of each member of the community and ensures that everyone is accounted for and ready to defend the community's honor.
  • Chronarch (Rin Tohsaka) - The one who is responsible for maintaining and organizing the community's database of information, including member profiles, organizational data, and other relevant records. They ensure that the database is up-to-date and accurate, and that access to sensitive information is appropriately restricted. The Chronarch also works closely with other Vindicators to provide data-driven insights and support decision-making processes, using their expertise to help guide the community towards its goals.
  • Scriptforge (Karma Akabane) - The Scriptforge is a vindicator of the community who possesses extensive programming experience and expertise. Their primary responsibility is to safeguard and maintain the community's codebase and technical infrastructure. The Scriptforge ensures that the community's software applications, platforms, and systems are secure, optimized, and up-to-date. They may review code contributions, conduct code audits, implement best practices, and collaborate with other community members to enhance the technical capabilities of the community.
  • Sagewisdom (Ishigami Senku) - Sagewisdom's role within the Vindicators is responsible for providing guidance, support, and knowledge to other members in their gaming endeavors. Sagewisdom is an experienced player who has a deep understanding of the games played within the community. He possesses extensive knowledge of game mechanics, strategies, and tactics.

Rules

  1. The use of languages other than English is prohibited in the GC. Failure to comply may result in deduction of points.
  2. Prior approval from the Dominaes is required before making changes to your nickname.
  3. Avoid engaging in conversation regarding sensitive topics, including religion, gender, race/nationality, politics, and explicit sexual content or R-18 material.
  4. It is important to only include individuals who have gone through the recruitment process as members, and only the Talonarch has the authority to add them.
  5. Avoid chatting in the main GC after a Dominae or Vindicators has instructed to turn off chat. You may only resume chatting after they have indicated to turn chat back on.
